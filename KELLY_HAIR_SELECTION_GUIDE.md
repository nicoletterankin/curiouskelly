## Step 1: Understand Kelly's Hair Requirements

### 1.1 Target Specifications

Based on reference images and character specification:

**Color:**
- **Base Color:** Medium brown (#5D4037 RGB: 93, 64, 55)
- **Highlights:** Caramel/honey-blonde (#D4A574 RGB: 212, 165, 116)
- **Distribution:** Subtle highlights throughout, not streaks

**Style:**
- **Texture:** Wavy to slightly curly
- **NOT:** Frizzy, whispy, tight curls, or straight
- **Cohesion:** Hair moves as cohesive wave sections
- **Finish:** Smooth, polished texture

**Length:**
- Shoulder-length or longer
- Cascades over shoulders

**Parting:**
- Slightly off-center or down the middle
- NOT side part

**Volume:**
- Rich and voluminous
- Not flat or thin

**Quality:**
- HD hair library (not basic hair)
- Individual strands visible
- Realistic texture

### 1.2 Visual Reference

**Key Characteristics to Match:**
- ✅ Medium brown base with warm undertones
- ✅ Subtle caramel/honey highlights (not obvious streaks)
- ✅ Wavy pattern (not curly, not straight)
- ✅ Hair strands stay together (cohesive sections)
- ✅ Shoulder-length or longer
- ✅ Rich volume
- ✅ Smooth, polished appearance

**What to Avoid:**
- ❌ Pure brown (no highlights)
- ❌ Blonde or light hair
- ❌ Black hair
- ❌ Frizzy or whispy texture
- ❌ Tight curls
- ❌ Short hair
- ❌ Side part
- ❌ Flat or thin appearance

## Step 2: Browse CC5 Hair Library

### 2.1 Access Hair Library

1. **Open CC5:**
   - Launch Character Creator 5
   - Open your Kelly project

2. **Access Hair Library:**
   - Go to **Content** tab (top menu)
   - Select **Hair** category
   - Or: **Character → Hair** from menu

3. **Filter Options:**
   - Use filters to narrow search:
     - **Quality:** HD (High Definition)
     - **Length:** Shoulder Length, Long
     - **Style:** Wavy, Curly (but not tight curls)
     - **Color:** Brown (we'll adjust color later)

### 2.2 Search Terms

**Recommended Search Terms:**
- "HD Wavy Hair"
- "HD Shoulder Length"
- "HD Medium Brown"
- "HD Long Wavy"
- "HD Natural Waves"

**Browse Categories:**
- HD Hair → Female → Long
- HD Hair → Female → Wavy
- Popular/Featured HD Hair

### 2.3 Preview Hair Assets

1. **View Previews:**
   - Click on hair asset to preview
   - View from multiple angles
   - Check texture detail

2. **Compare to Reference:**
   - Have reference images open side-by-side
   - Compare:
     - Wave pattern
     - Length
     - Overall style
     - Volume

3. **Take Notes:**
   - Document asset names that look promising
   - Note asset IDs or purchase requirements
   - Create shortlist of 3-5 candidates

## Step 4: Purchase Considerations

### 4.1 CC5 Hair Assets

**Free Assets:**
- Some hair assets included with CC5
- Check "Free" or "Included" filter
- May be sufficient for testing

**Paid Assets:**
- CC5 Marketplace assets
- Third-party hair packs
- Individual hair assets

**Cost Considerations:**
- Individual assets: $5-20 typically
- Hair packs: $30-100 (multiple styles)
- Evaluate cost vs. quality match

**Recommendation:**
- Start with free/included assets
- Test on Kelly model first
- If no good match, purchase individual assets
- Avoid buying packs unless multiple styles needed

### 4.2 Alternative Sources

**If CC5 library insufficient:**
- **Reallusion Content Store:** Official CC5 marketplace
- **Third-party vendors:** Check compatibility
- **Custom hair:** May require external tools

**Verify Compatibility:**
- Ensure asset works with CC5
- Check version compatibility
- Read reviews/ratings

## Step 5: Apply Hair to Kelly Model

### 5.1 Select Hair Asset

1. **Choose from shortlist:**
   - Select top candidate from Step 3
   - Or test multiple candidates

2. **Load Hair:**
   - Click on hair asset
   - Select **Apply** or **Load**
   - Hair should appear on Kelly

### 5.2 Adjust Hair Position

1. **Check Attachment:**
   - Verify hair attaches properly to scalp
   - Look for gaps or misalignment
   - Rotate view to check all angles

2. **Adjust Position:**
   - **Hair → Position** (if available)
   - Adjust:
     - Forward/backward
     - Up/down
     - Rotation
   - Ensure natural placement

3. **Check Parting:**
   - Verify parting matches reference
   - Adjust if needed (center or slightly off-center)
   - Avoid side part

### 5.3 Initial Fit Check

1. **Visual Inspection:**
   - Compare to reference images
   - Check length, volume, style
   - Note what needs adjustment

2. **Take Screenshot:**
   - Save as: `kelly_hair_candidate_1.png`
   - Document candidate name

3. **Repeat for Other Candidates:**
   - Test all shortlisted candidates
   - Compare side-by-side
   - Select best match

## Step 6: Color Adjustment

### 6.1 Access Hair Color Tools

1. **Select Hair:**
   - Ensure hair is selected in CC5

2. **Open Hair Material/Color Editor:**
   - **Modify → Hair** (or Hair tab)
   - Look for **Color**, **Material**, or **Hair Color** options

### 6.2 Adjust Base Color

1. **Set Base Color:**
   - Target: Medium brown (#5D4037 RGB: 93, 64, 55)
   - Use color picker or RGB sliders
   - Adjust until matches reference

2. **Check Warmth:**
   - Ensure warm undertones (not cool brown)
   - Compare to reference image
   - Adjust hue if needed

### 6.3 Add Highlights

1. **Enable Highlight System:**
   - Look for **Highlights**, **Secondary Color**, or **Highlight Color**
   - Enable if available

2. **Set Highlight Color:**
   - Target: Caramel/honey-blonde (#D4A574 RGB: 212, 165, 116)
   - Adjust color picker

3. **Adjust Highlight Distribution:**
   - **Highlight Amount/Intensity:** Subtle (not obvious streaks)
   - **Highlight Distribution:** Throughout hair (not just tips)
   - Compare to reference: highlights should be subtle

4. **Fine-tune:**
   - Compare to reference images
   - Adjust until matches
   - Should look natural, not dyed

### 6.4 Final Color Check

1. **Compare to Reference:**
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - Check in different lighting
   - Verify matches reference

2. **Save Color Preset:**
   - Save hair color settings if possible
   - Name: "Kelly_Hair_Color"
   - Use for future adjustments

## Step 7: Physics Setup

### 7.1 Access Physics Settings

1. **Select Hair:**
   - Ensure hair is selected

2. **Open Physics/Simulation Settings:**
   - **Hair → Physics** (or similar)
   - Look for **Soft Cloth**, **Hair Physics**, or **Simulation** options

### 7.2 Configure Physics

**Target Specifications:**
- **Amplitude:** ≤0.3cm movement
- **Natural Movement:** Realistic hair physics
- **No Clipping:** Hair doesn't intersect with body

**Settings to Adjust:**
1. **Gravity:** Default (usually fine)
2. **Elasticity:** 0.65 (moderate)
3. **Damping:** 0.45 (moderate)
4. **Air Resistance:** 0.08 (light)
5. **Self-Collision:** Enabled
6. **Amplitude/Limit:** ≤0.3cm

**CC5 Specific Settings:**
- Follow CC5 hair physics documentation
- Use presets if available
- Adjust incrementally

### 7.3 Test Physics

1. **Preview Animation:**
   - Play animation or move character
   - Observe hair movement
   - Check for clipping

2. **Adjust if Needed:**
   - Reduce amplitude if too much movement
   - Increase damping if too bouncy
   - Fix clipping if present

3. **Save Physics Preset:**
   - Save as: `Kelly_Hair_Physics.json` (if exportable)
   - Or document settings for reference

## Troubleshooting

### Issue: No hair assets match reference
**Solution:**
- Try color adjustment first (may make acceptable asset match)
- Consider purchasing individual assets
- Look for similar styles that can be adjusted
- Check third-party vendors

### Issue: Hair doesn't attach properly
**Solution:**
- Check hair is designed for CC5
- Verify CC5 version compatibility
- Adjust position manually
- Try different hair assets

### Issue: Color adjustment insufficient
**Solution:**
- Check if hair asset supports color adjustment
- Try different base color
- Adjust highlights separately
- Consider texture painting if available

### Issue: Physics too bouncy or not moving
**Solution:**
- Adjust damping (higher = less bouncy)
- Adjust amplitude (lower = less movement)
- Check gravity settings
- Verify physics enabled

### Issue: Hair clips through body
**Solution:**
- Adjust collision settings
- Reduce physics amplitude
- Adjust hair position
- Check for mesh issues

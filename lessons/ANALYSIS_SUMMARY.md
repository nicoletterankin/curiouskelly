## Age-Less Universal Topic Checklist

A topic is truly "age-less" if:
- ✅ Can be experienced by ages 2-102
- ✅ Has meaning at multiple complexity levels
- ✅ Connects to universal human experiences
- ✅ Inspires wonder across all ages
- ✅ Is culturally universal
- ✅ Is observable in nature or daily life

**Good Examples:** Sun, Moon, Rain, Friendship, Kindness, Colors, Shadows  
**Poor Examples:** Innovation Networks, Prototyping, Patent System

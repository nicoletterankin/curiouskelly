# Technology Lessons Analysis - Summary for 2026+

## Quick Facts

- **Total Tech Lessons:** 256 (70.1% of all lessons)
- **High Value (2026+):** 221 (86.3%) ✅
- **Medium Value:** 27 (10.5%)
- **Low Value:** 8 (3.1%) ⚠️
- **Innovation Lessons:** 26 (too many, need consolidation)
- **Average Age-Less Score:** 1.66/4 (most NOT truly age-less)

## The Good News ✅

1. **86.3% are high value** - Most tech lessons are valuable for 2026+
2. **Foundational computing lessons are excellent** - Computer Science, AI, etc.
3. **Critical thinking lessons are strong** - Media Studies, Logic, etc.
4. **Sustainable tech is well-covered** - Environmental Innovation, etc.

## The Issues ⚠️

1. **26 innovation lessons = redundancy**
   - Many are process/methodology focused
   - Not truly age-less (too abstract)
   - Could consolidate to 8-10 core lessons

2. **Only 1.66/4 age-less score**
   - Most tech lessons aren't truly universal
   - Hard for 3-year-olds and 80-year-olds to relate
   - Too abstract, not observable

3. **70.1% tech focus = too heavy**
   - Needs balance with natural phenomena
   - Needs balance with universal human experiences

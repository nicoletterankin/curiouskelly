## ðŸ”§ **TROUBLESHOOTING 8K QUALITY**

### **Issue: Still 2K Quality**
**Solutions**:
1. **Check SubD levels** - must be at 4
2. **Verify export settings** - must be Ultra High
3. **Confirm render resolution** - must be 7680x4320
4. **Check hair quality** - use HD hair systems

### **Issue: Hair Not Visible**
**Solutions**:
1. **Ensure hair is applied** after head generation
2. **Check hair visibility** in viewport
3. **Verify hair export** settings
4. **Try different hair systems** if needed

### **Issue: Slow Performance**
**Solutions**:
1. **Reduce viewport SubD** to 2-3 for editing
2. **Use render SubD** at 4 for final output
3. **Close other applications** during render
4. **Consider GPU acceleration** if available

### **Issue: Export Fails**
**Solutions**:
1. **Reduce SubD temporarily** for export
2. **Check available disk space** (8K needs lots of space)
3. **Export without hair first**, then add hair in iClone
4. **Use lower quality** for initial testing

## üöÄ STEP-BY-STEP WORKFLOW

### **PHASE 1: Prepare Kelly Photo (5 minutes)**

1. **Locate your Runway AI Kelly headshot**
   - Should be high resolution (preferably 4K+)
   - Front-facing, good lighting
   - Clear facial features
   - Save as: `projects/Kelly/Ref/kelly_headshot.jpg`

2. **Prepare audio file**
   - Use existing: `projects/Kelly/Audio/kelly25_audio.wav`
   - Or create new test audio for lipsync

### **PHASE 2: CC5 Headshot 2 Setup (15 minutes)**

1. **Launch Character Creator 5**
2. **Go to Headshot 2 tab**
3. **Click "Settings" or "Advanced Options"**
4. **Set MAXIMUM quality settings:**
   - Resolution: "Ultra High" or "8K"
   - Detail Level: "Maximum"
   - Texture Quality: "8K" or "Ultra High"
   - Mesh Density: "Maximum"
   - Processing Quality: "Ultra High"

5. **Load Kelly Photo:**
   - Click "Load Photo"
   - Select `projects/Kelly/Ref/kelly_headshot.jpg`
   - Advanced Settings:
     - Gender: Female
     - Age Range: 25-35
     - Skin Tone: Match photo exactly
     - Facial Features: "High Detail"
     - Expression: "Neutral" or "Slight Smile"

6. **Generate Ultra-High Quality Head:**
   - Click "Generate"
   - **WAIT 10-15 minutes** for ultra-high processing
   - Review generated head (should show much more detail)
   - Click "Apply to Character"
   - Wait for high-resolution transfer (2-3 minutes)
   - Click "Accept" when satisfied

### **PHASE 3: Maximum Subdivision (10 minutes)**

1. **Switch to "Modify" tab**
2. **In "SubD Levels" section:**
   - Viewport: Set to "4" (maximum)
   - Render: Set to "4" (maximum)
3. **Click "Subdivide" button**
4. **Wait for maximum subdivision** (5-10 minutes)

5. **Enable All Detail Features:**

     - ‚úÖ "Corrective Expressions" (checked)

     - ‚úÖ "Show Subdivision"
     - ‚úÖ "Show Details" 
     - ‚úÖ "High Quality Preview"

# Image Generation Prompts: The Rein Maker's Daughter
**Version:** 1.0

This document contains the specific, detailed prompts for generating key style frames and visual targets for the cinematic. Each prompt is designed to be used with a photorealistic, diffusion-based image generation model.

### **Shot 01: The Hammer Spark**
-   **Storyboard:** "From pure black, a sudden, explosive flash of white-hot sparks erupts in the center of the frame, revealing the silhouette of a heavy hammer mid-strike. The sparks fly outwards and quickly fade, leaving dying embers."
-   **Prompt:** `cinematic still, photorealistic, ultra-detailed, from pitch black, a super slow-motion macro shot of a heavy, sleek, steel hammer striking an unseen surface, creating an explosive burst of white-hot sparks that fly towards the camera, dramatic high-contrast lighting, shallow depth of field, embers glowing with residual heat, mood is powerful and precise.`

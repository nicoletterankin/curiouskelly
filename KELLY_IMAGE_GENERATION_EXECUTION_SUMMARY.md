## üîç Brand Alignment Checklist

### Character Consistency (Non-Negotiable)
- [ ] Face shape: Oval (not round, not square)
- [ ] Skin tone: Warm light-medium (not pale, not dark, not cool-toned)
- [ ] Eyes: Warm brown, almond-shaped (not blue, not round)
- [ ] Hair: Medium brown with caramel/honey-blonde highlights (not pure brown, not blonde)
- [ ] Hair texture: Soft wavy to slightly curly (not straight, not tight curls)
- [ ] Expression: Genuine warm smile with straight white teeth (not closed mouth, not fake)
- [ ] Age: Late 20s to early 30s (not younger, not older)
- [ ] Build: Athletic, strong capable presence (not thin, not heavy)

### Wardrobe Variants
- [ ] **Reinmaker:** Dark gray, charcoal, metallic steel ONLY (no bright colors, no reds/yellows)
- [ ] **Daily Lesson:** Light blue ribbed knit sweater, director's chair, white background

### Style & Quality
- [ ] Photorealistic digital human (not cartoon, not stylized)
- [ ] Professional photography quality (not game asset, not CGI)
- [ ] High detail, realistic textures (not low-res, not painted)
- [ ] No artifacts, blur, compression, watermarks

**All items must pass for "Insanely Great" rating.**
